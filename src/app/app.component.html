Company Profile


MC Square is an Information Technology service provider and consulting firm that serves top organizations to do business better and realize their goals by leveraging our industry-wide experience, deep technology expertise, portfolio of services and vertically aligned business model. Years of experience enable us to harness the latest technology for delivering business capability to our clients

MC Square has been providing business technology solutions that empower our clients to realize the full potential from their enterprise data. We provide business consulting, software solutions, mobile app services, big data expertise, training and placements that increase operational efficiency, enhance enterprise collaboration and enable faster decision-making to achieve operational performance. We are dedicated to helping organizations become more efficient, profitable, and competitive.

At MC Square, we are committed to pioneering a new approach to business consulting. Our philosophy and reason for success is that we believe power should be returned to the client. We work closely with our clients to meet their specific needs in designing and implementing flexible, scalable and easy-to-use solutions.

We are experts in designing, building and delivering business-driven technology solutions. We help our clients gain competitive advantage by using Internet-based technologies to make their businesses more responsive to market opportunities and threats, strengthen relationships with customers, suppliers and partners, improve productivity and reduce information technology costs.
